About Us

Prowess Technologies PVT (LTD) is an IT Solution Company in Zimbabwe. We provide both Computer products and Computer Services to Small, Medium and Large Enterprises. We provide total IT Support and Solutions around Southern Africa. Our team has the expertise and the understanding of what is required to deliver quality and reliable services from Help Desk Remote Support, Onsite Support, Networking, Data Recovery, Customized Application Development, CCTV, Access control, Printing Solutions, to Complete Outsourcing of IT Needs for any organization. You can let us take care of your IT Department while you are focusing on your business.
